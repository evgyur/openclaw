## Strategy (Nikita Bier, Head of Product Twitter)

**Medium form wins:**
- 1-2 paragraphs = sweet spot
- Short enough to hook, long enough to explain
- Too short = no depth, too long = people leave

**Niche down for monetization:**
- Pick ONE subject you know deeply (from real experience)
- Post insights from that niche daily for 6+ months
- Under 5 sentences per post
- Algorithm promotes consistent niche experts → brands find you

**Long game:**
- Most quit after 1-2 months (no results yet)
- Winners post 1-2 years nonstop
- X will promote your account if you stick to it
- Result: recognized expert → charge for endorsements/time
